The Tale of Storage Hub

In a world of bits and bytes so vast,
Lived little files, from first to last.
They needed homes, safe places to stay,
But the old storage barn was wearing away.

Along came StorageHub, a protocol so bright,
With a plan to keep files safe day and night.
"Don't worry," said StorageHub with a digital grin,
"I'll spread you out: safe, wide and thin!"

The jpg smiled, the pdf beamed,
This new idea was better than they'd dreamed.
"But how?" asked a curious little txt,
"Will you keep us safe and never perplexed?"

StorageHub explained with a twinkle and a laugh,
"I'll split you up, not by half, but by half of half!
Across the network, you'll be spread about,
Safe and sound, without a doubt!"

The files cheered, from doc to exe,
This plan seemed smart and rather sexy.
No single point of failure, no central space,
Just bits and pieces all over the place.

So StorageHub got to work with all his might,
Scattering data left and right.
A piece here, a piece there,
Files everywhere, but with great care.

And when a user came to look,
For a photo, a song, or a favorite book,
StorageHub would gather the pieces, quick as can be,
Reassembling files for all to see.

The old storage barn stood empty and bare,
But nobody minded, nobody cared.
For Storage Hub had saved the day,
Keeping files safe in a whole new way.

So remember, dear children, when you save or share,
Your files are protected with Storage Hub's care.
Decentralized, distributed, and always in reach,
That's the lesson that Storage Hub can teach!
